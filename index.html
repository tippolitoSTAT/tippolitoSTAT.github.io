Thomas E. Ippolito, P.h.D. MBA is a Lecturer in the University of Florida Department of Statistics. Dr. Ippolito specializes in financial statistics and time series causal inference and is a leading expert in estimating and forecasting the effects of events on financial securities. He completed his Ph.D. in statistics and data science at Northwestern University where he was advised by Larry Hedges, Board of Trustees Professor of Statistics.

Dr. Ippolito previously worked in managment consulting, investment banking, and is a veteran of the United States Marine Corps.

Expertise:

  • Time Series Causal Inference
  • Financial Statistics
  • Forecasting Methods
  • Data Science
